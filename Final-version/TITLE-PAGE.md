## AI-use disclosure

Large language models (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI GPT) were used for: (i) development of the Python analysis scripts, all outputs verified by the lead author against the data; and (ii) an automated, construction-year-blinded coding-reproducibility check of the classification (reported in Data and Methods). The models are not authors. This is disclosed here and in the manuscript's Methods.
