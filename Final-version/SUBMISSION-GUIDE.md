# Step-by-step submission guide — *Fire Technology* (Springer Nature)

> **Verified facts (Springer primary source, 2026-07-03):**
> - Fire Technology has **no "Short Communication" type**. Its types are **Research, Review, Matters Arising, Case Study, Database**. Submit under **Research** (there is no word limit for Research; ~4,300 words is fine; abstract must be 150–250 words — ours is 233).
> - The journal is **DOUBLE-ANONYMOUS**: submit the **anonymized manuscript** + a **separate Title Page**. Both are in this folder.
> - Submission is via **SNAPP**, not Editorial Manager (the old EM site is dead).
> - References are **numbered [n] (Vancouver)** — the manuscript already renders this way.
> - As a **UNAK** corresponding author you get the **~€2,590 APC waived** under the Iceland Springer read-and-publish agreement.

Everything you need is in this `Final-version/` folder. Work top to bottom; it takes about 30–40 minutes once Tom has signed off.
